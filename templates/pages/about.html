About Us

What is Signsense?

Signsense is a web application that aims to bridge the communication gap between the hearing and the deaf community. It is designed to help the hearing community learn sign language and communicate with the deaf community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create an inclusive world where communication barriers between the hearing and the deaf are eliminated through innovative technology.
