Welcome to Signsense

Bridging the communication gap between the hearing and the deaf community

Get Started

How does it work?

Signsense uses a webcam to detect the user's hand gestures and translates them into text.

Why use Signsense?

Signsense is a free and easy-to-use web application accessible from any device.

Who can use Signsense?

Designed for anyone who wants to learn sign language or communicate with the deaf community.

How can I support Signsense?

Help us spread the word, share it with friends and family, and provide feedback.
